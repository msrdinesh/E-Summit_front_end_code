E-summit, as a culture of IIT Bhubaneswar, has proved its worth in depleting the Chasm that exists between Entrepreneurship and management. "Where Investors Meets Innovators"

Director's Message


IIT Bhubaneswar is a premier Institution in India which continuously strives for Entrepreneurship Development in the Society. Entrepreneurship Summit, a three day annual Entrepreneurship conclave of IIT Bhubaneswar, plays a key role in passing core values to the society. E-Summit as a culture of IIT Bhubaneswar has proved its worth in infusing a sense of excitement in students in innovation & invention, design & creation and entrepreneurship at Odisha. Putting forward the Theme "Entrepreneurship & Innovation in the Digital Era", IIT Bhubaneswar made a remarkable attempt to take students into the Entrepreneurial world and encourage them to be Entrepreneurs.

- Prof.Ratnam.V.Raja Kumar
Director, IIT Bhubaneswar

Chairman's Message


E-Summit at IIT Bhubaneswar provides a platform for the interaction among venture capitalists, seasoned & upcoming entreprenuers, academia and younger minds. The Summit involves participation from esteemed business leaders and provides an opportunity for the startups to access IIT Bhubaneswar's talent pool. E-Summit includes vision night in which experienced delegates from various fields will motivate young work force towards initiating their entrepreneur journey.
We Cordially invite you, your family and friends to various events of E-Summit-2018

- Dr. Rajan Jha
Chairman, E-Summit'18

THEME

>

We are in a new Era that builds and extends the impact of digitization in new and unanticipated ways. This wave of digitalization creates numerous opportunities for innovative entrepreneurs. Many new ventures grasp the opportunity introducing disruptive business models to the market.

Digital transformation was important for every industry, because innovative digital business models also attacked traditional industries. No industry could escape this trend digitalization is becoming horizontal and crosscutting and affecting all sectors of our economy, society and industry.
